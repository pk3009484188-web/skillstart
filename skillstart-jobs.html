📋 Data Entry 🎓 Matric / Inter 🕐 Full Time 📍 Lahore, Punjab ⭐ Featured
📋 Job Overview

We are looking for a motivated and reliable Data Entry Operator to join our growing team in Lahore. This is an entry-level position suitable for fresh candidates with basic computer knowledge. No prior experience is required — we will train the right candidate.

In-content 300×250 AdSense Rectangle
📝 Responsibilities
  • Enter and update data in company systems and spreadsheets
  • Verify accuracy of data before submission
  • Maintain organised digital and physical records
  • Communicate updates to supervisor via WhatsApp or email
  • Complete daily targets within office hours
✅ Requirements
  • Matric or Intermediate qualification (any board)
  • Basic typing speed (30+ WPM preferred but not mandatory)
  • Familiarity with MS Office or Google Sheets is a plus
  • Honest, punctual, and willing to learn
  • Residents of Lahore preferred
🎁 Benefits
  • 35,000 – 45,000 PKR per month (based on performance)
  • On-the-job training provided
  • Eid bonus and annual increment
  • Friendly, professional work environment
📬 How to Apply
  • Send your CV to the email address listed above
  • Use subject line: "Data Entry — SkillStart Jobs"
  • WhatsApp applications also accepted at the number provided
  • Deadline: April 30, 2026 — apply early
