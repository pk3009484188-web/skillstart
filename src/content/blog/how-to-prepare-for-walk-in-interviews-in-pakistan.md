Walk-in interviews move fast, so preparation is essential.

## Checklist

- 3 printed CV copies
- Original and copy of CNIC
- Educational documents
- Pen and small notepad

## Day-of Tips

- Reach 20-30 minutes early
- Dress neat and simple
- Practice short introduction

Stay calm and answer honestly.
