Small errors can block your selection even when jobs are available.

## Common Mistakes

1. Applying without reading requirements
2. Sending outdated CV
3. Missing interview calls
4. Weak communication in interviews
5. Late arrival
6. Incomplete applications
7. Fake information in CV
8. No follow-up
9. Poor email subject lines
10. Applying inconsistently

Fix these basics to improve your hiring results.
