Typing improvement is a major advantage for data entry hiring.

## Daily Plan (20-30 Minutes)

- 10 minutes typing drills
- 10 minutes accuracy practice
- 5 minutes number entry

## Benchmarks

- Beginner: 20-30 WPM
- Good starting level: 30-40 WPM
- Strong level: 40+ WPM with accuracy

Focus on fewer errors, not only speed.
