Fake ads waste time and money. Always verify before applying.

## Red Flags

- Upfront payment demand
- No company address
- Unclear job description
- Suspicious WhatsApp-only contact

## Verification Steps

- Check official website
- Search company on maps and social pages
- Confirm recruiter email domain
- Never share sensitive documents early

## Safety Rule

Real employers do not ask for money to offer a job.
