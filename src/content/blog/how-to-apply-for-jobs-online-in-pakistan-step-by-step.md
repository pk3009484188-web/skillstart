Applying online is easy when you follow a structured process.

## Step-by-Step

1. Prepare your updated CV.
2. Search relevant job titles only.
3. Read eligibility before applying.
4. Submit complete details.
5. Save the job link and date.

## After Applying

- Check email daily.
- Keep phone active.
- Prepare interview answers.

## Avoid These Errors

- Applying to unrelated roles
- Sending incomplete forms
- Using unprofessional email addresses
