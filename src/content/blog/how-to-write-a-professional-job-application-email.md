A clean application email can create a strong first impression.

## Email Structure

- Subject: Job Title - Your Name
- Greeting: Dear Hiring Manager
- Intro: Role you are applying for
- Body: 2-3 lines on your fit
- Closing: Thank you + contact number

## Example Subject

`Application for Data Entry Operator - Ahmed Ali`

Attach CV in PDF and keep the message concise.
