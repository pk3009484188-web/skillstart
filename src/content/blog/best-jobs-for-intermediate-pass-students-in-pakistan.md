Intermediate pass candidates can apply for office and support roles with strong growth potential.

## Recommended Roles

- Computer operator
- Customer support representative
- Sales support assistant
- Front desk officer
- Junior admin assistant

## What Employers Prefer

- Basic communication skills
- Computer familiarity
- Punctuality and reliability
- Willingness to learn

## Quick Action Plan

- Build a simple CV
- Apply to 5-10 relevant roles weekly
- Prepare for short interviews
