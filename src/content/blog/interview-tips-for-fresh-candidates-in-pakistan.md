Interview confidence grows with preparation.

## Before Interview

- Read job description carefully
- Practice self-introduction
- Prepare 3 strengths and 1 weakness
- Carry printed CV copies

## During Interview

- Speak clearly
- Keep answers short and relevant
- Be honest about experience level

## After Interview

Send a short thank-you message if possible.
