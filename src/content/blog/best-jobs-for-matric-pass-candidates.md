If you have completed Matric, you can still apply for many good private sector jobs in Pakistan.

## Top Roles to Target

- Data entry operator
- Office assistant
- Reception helper
- Store assistant
- Customer support trainee

## Skills That Increase Your Chances

- Basic typing speed
- WhatsApp and email usage
- Simple MS Word formatting
- Polite communication

## Where to Search

- Company career pages
- Trusted job websites
- Verified social media pages
- Local office notice boards

## Final Tip

Apply regularly and improve one skill every week. Consistency matters more than perfection.
